# DayMirror — Brand & positioning

> **Tagline:** See where your day actually went.
> **Category:** The Time Audit App (SEO-friendly positioning under the DayMirror brand)

## The one sentence that sells

Most productivity apps help you plan your day. DayMirror helps you understand the day you actually lived.

## Elevator pitch

The hourly reflection app that helps you understand how you actually spend your time—not just how you planned to.

## Homepage copy (locked for Phase 3 landing)

| Element | Copy |
|---|---|
| **Hero headline** | Stop guessing where your day went. |
| **Hero subhead** | DayMirror helps you reflect on every hour, compare your plans with reality, and build better days through awareness. |
| **Primary CTA** | Start Reflecting |
| **Secondary descriptor** | Your personal Time Audit. |
| **Meta description** | DayMirror — hourly reflection & productivity tracker. See where your day actually went. Close the gap between planning and reality. |

## Category language (use in SEO, not as product name)

Pick one primary category phrase everywhere:

- **The Time Audit App** ← recommended (memorable + searchable)
- The Time Reflection App
- The Daily Reflection Platform

App Store subtitle: **Hourly Reflection & Productivity Tracker** — or — **Reflect. Track. Improve.**

## Brand pillars (every feature must support these)

| Pillar | Question | Landing section angle |
|---|---|---|
| 🪞 **Reflect** | What actually happened? | Hourly tracker screenshots |
| 📊 **Understand** | Where did my time go? | Analytics / plan vs reality |
| 🌱 **Improve** | How can tomorrow be better? | Weekly patterns, gentle nudges |

Notice what's missing: **"manage."** Awareness first, not another task manager.

## What people actually search (SEO targets)

People don't search "hourly tracker." They search feelings and problems:

| Search intent | Example queries | DayMirror answer |
|---|---|---|
| **Time guilt** | "where did my day go", "how do I stop wasting time" | Hero: Stop guessing where your day went. |
| **Self-improvement** | "self improvement app", "personal growth", "daily reflection" | Category: Daily Reflection Platform |
| **Productivity** | "productivity app", "time management", "improve productivity" | Subtitle: Hourly Reflection & Productivity Tracker |
| **Habits & review** | "habit tracker", "daily review", "daily journal", "accountability app" | Content: "How to build a daily review habit" |
| **Time tracking** | "time tracker", "time audit", "time diary" | Category: The Time Audit App |
| **Planning** | "daily planner", "life planner", "routine tracker" | Supporting feature — planner is secondary |

### Metadata keywords (site + App Store)

```
productivity, self improvement, daily reflection, time tracker, hour tracker,
journal, planner, daily review, time audit, focus, personal growth,
```

5. **Positioning** — "Most apps help you plan…" quote block
6. **Features** — Tracker (hero), Planner (supporting), Analytics (insights)
7. **Social proof** — Beta quotes (empty until Phase 4)
8. **FAQ** — Question-shaped H2s for AEO (see ROADMAP Phase 3)
