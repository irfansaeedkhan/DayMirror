# DayMirror — Brand & positioning

> Tagline: **See where your day actually went.**

## Elevator pitch

The hourly reflection app that helps you understand how you actually spend your time—not just how you planned to.

## Homepage hero

**Stop guessing where your day went.**

DayMirror helps you reflect on every hour, compare your plans with reality, and build better days through awareness.

**CTA:** Start Reflecting

## Positioning statement

Most productivity apps help you plan your day. DayMirror helps you understand the day you actually lived.

## Category

Not "another productivity app." Position as:

- **The Time Reflection App**
- **The Time Audit App**
- Or: **The Daily Reflection Platform**

## Brand pillars (every feature must support these)

| Pillar | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🪞 **Reflect** | What actually happened? |
| 📊 **Understand** | Where did my time go? |
| 🌱 **Improve** | How can tomorrow be better? |

Notice what's missing: **"manage."** Awareness first, not another task manager.

## App store copy (future)

- **Subtitle:** Hourly Reflection & Productivity Tracker — or — Reflect. Track. Improve.

## Logo direction

Not a literal mirror. Consider: clock face + subtle symmetry/reflection + timeline. Minimal, clean.

## Design reference

UI/landing work follows `DESIGN-ETHICS.md` (Sunsama calm aesthetic as benchmark).
